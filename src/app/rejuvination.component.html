Rejuvenation treatment is a broad term for cosmetic procedures that aim to improve the appearance of skin by treating signs of aging and damage, such as wrinkles, scars, and sunspots. These treatments work by stimulating the growth of new, healthy skin and boosting collagen production, and they can range from non-invasive methods like laser therapy and chemical peels to more invasive surgical options.
